Welcome

Welcome to the Moon Landing Museum!

Who are we

On July 20th 1969 Neil Armstrong took man’s first steps on the Moon, it was an historic event which captivated the world. Here at the museum we showcase all things Apollo 11

What's here

  • The actual Apollo 11 Command Module Columbia AND the Lunar Landing Pod, The Eagle
  • Over 20 one of a kind artifacts
  • Dozens of NASA and Russian Spaceflight Additions

Our website

Take a tour round our website where we’ll take you through the timeline of the Mission and introduce you to the three men whose names have gone down in history; astronauts Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin and Michael Collins. Our 'Visit Us' page will give you all the information on how to find us. For any Apollo 11 enthusiasts out there who are interested in volunteering with us, take a look at the Volunteering page where you can register your interest.
